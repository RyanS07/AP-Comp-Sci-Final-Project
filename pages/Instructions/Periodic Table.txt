Select elements by clicking on their icon. Once all desired elements are selected, press "See Details". 
 - Red Icons = Metals (Alkali Metals, Alkaline Earth Metals, Transitional Metals, Post-Transitional Metals)
 - Green Icons = Gases (Nonmetals, Halogens, Noble Gases)
 - Yellow Icons = Metalloids
 - Grey Icons = Other (Lanthanoids, Actinoids)
